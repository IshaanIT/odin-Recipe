Rocky Mountain Oysters

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound bull or pig testicles
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 cup milk
  • Vegetable oil for frying
Instructions:
  1. Clean and prepare the testicles: Start by washing the testicles under cold running water and removing the outer skin. Then, slice the testicles into thin, round slices about 1/4 inch thick.
  2. Soak the testicles in milk: Place the sliced testicles in a bowl and pour enough milk over them to cover them completely. Let them soak for at least 1 hour. This helps to remove any unwanted flavors and to tenderize the meat.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients: In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, and paprika.
  4. Coat the testicles in the flour mixture: Remove the testicles from the milk and dredge them in the flour mixture, making sure to coat them thoroughly.
  5. Fry the testicles: Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or heavy skillet over medium-high heat until it reaches 375F. Carefully add the testicles to the hot oil and fry them until they are golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes.
  6. Drain and serve: Remove the fried testicles from the oil and drain them on a paper towel. Serve them hot with your favorite dipping sauce, such as ranch dressing or hot sauce.
Note: Rocky Mountain Oysters can also be grilled, baked, or sauted instead of deep-fried.
