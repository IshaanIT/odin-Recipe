\\\\\FOIE GRAS////>

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 lb fresh duck or goose liver
    • 2 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp white pepper
    • 2 tbsp cognac or brandy
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1/4 cup white wine
    • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Instructions:
    1. Preheat oven to 350F (175C).
    2. Clean the liver of any veins or impurities and sprinkle with salt and white pepper.
    3. Place the liver in a terrine or oven-proof dish and pour cognac or brandy over it.
    4. Sprinkle with sugar and pour white wine over the liver.
    5. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for about 30 minutes.
    6. Remove the foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
    7. Let the foie gras cool to room temperature, then refrigerate until ready to serve.
    8. Serve with crusty bread, crackers, or fruit.
