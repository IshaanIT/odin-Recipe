\\\\\FRIED TARANTULAS////>

  • 4-6 tarantulas
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • Bamboo skewers (optional)
  1. Remove the fangs, venom glands, and hairs from the tarantulas. If you are not sure how to do this, it's best to purchase cleaned tarantulas from a reputable seller.
  2. Mix the salt, sugar, and garlic powder in a bowl.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan until it reaches 375F (190C).
  4. Roll the tarantulas in the spice mixture, making sure they are fully coated.
  5. If using skewers, gently pierce the tarantulas through the abdomen and thorax, leaving the legs and head free.
  6. Carefully place the tarantulas in the hot oil and fry them for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Remove the tarantulas from the oil and place them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Serve immediately with your favorite dipping sauce.
Note: Some people prefer to remove the abdomen and legs before frying the tarantulas, but this is a matter of personal preference. While fried tarantulas may not be for everyone, they are considered a delicacy in certain parts of the world. If you decide to give this recipe a try, make sure to purchase your tarantulas from a reputable source and follow food safety guidelines when preparing and cooking them.
